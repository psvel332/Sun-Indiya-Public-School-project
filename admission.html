Admission Procedure

Play School

  • Parents who require admissions to their wards in the Play school, please contact the office between January second week and fourth week every year.

    Classes I to V

    Admissions for classes I to V are newly open in the senior school also in the academic year 2022- 2023.

    Admissions for classes VI to IX and XI are open for day scholars as well as boarders from the academic year 2022-2023.

    Classes VI to IX and XI

    Admissions for Class VI to IX and XI are open for day scholars as well as boarders and in available vacancies. Admissions are finalized on the basis of their performance in the written test in Tamil, English and Mathematics to be conducted in the first week of April every year. For Std XI, new admissions are made on the basis of the marks obtained in Std X Board Examinations.

    Parents who require admissions to their wards in classes VI to IX and XI may submit the filled in application form to the Principal to register for admissions from the third week of January every year. The Principal reserves the right to admit or reject any application for admission without assigning any reason.
