Our Facilities

Transport

School children using school transport are expected to be escorted to and from their pickup points, failing which the child will be dropped at school.

Language

Provides all students, no matter wherever they are seated in the classroom, equal opportunity in the four basic requirements, Listening, Speaking, Reading and Writing

Library

School also subscribes for leading magazines and dailies. Internet facility is also available for reference purpose. A full time librarian is available
